PROJECT TOPI

Project Topi, a welfare society established in 2000 a platform within the GIK community It executes numerous events and modules annually, a significant part of these have been conducted successfully and that too with impactful consequences on people living nearby and within GIK. As the years pass by every module is improved in various ways or new ones introduced. Because of this cause Many daily wagers around GIK are now able to cope up with their daily life expenses and educate their children.

Sponsor A Child

Last year we initiated the “Sponsor a Child Program”, the purpose was to sponsor the education of those students who are not financially stable. The overwhelming response from our internal and external donors made it possible for 60 students to continue their education in schools under this program.

Mess Bill Aid

Every year Project Topi receives applications from 60-70 students who are not able to pay their mess bills due to financial conditions. With the support of the Giki administration, we pay the mess bills of 20 students partially every year. This year Project Topi launched the “MESS BILL AID PROGRAM”. Under this program, we aim to pay the mess bill of all students who are deserving but aren’t entertained due to limited resources.

Blood Donation

Blood is one of the essential fluid in our body, which helps the body to function smoothly. Blood Donation is an act of donating healthy blood to needy people. Due to excessive loss of blood, a person may die. Therefore, we can say that blood donation is an act of saving lives.
